StatsDeck ("we," "our") is an add-on (an MCP connector) for Claude that answers fantasy football questions using NFL statistics and, if you connect one, your fantasy league data. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Account email and ID. When you connect StatsDeck, you sign in with Google. We store your email address and a unique account identifier so we can recognize you across sessions.
  • Usage events. We record which StatsDeck features you use and when, tied to your account ID, so we can keep the service reliable and understand what's useful.
  • League data you choose to connect. If you link a fantasy league (e.g. Sleeper), we read public league information — your roster, league scoring settings, and available players — to answer your questions. We do not collect login passwords for these platforms.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not post, change, or delete anything in your fantasy leagues — StatsDeck only reads.
  • We do not provide betting odds or lines, and we do not collect payment card details directly (any future paid plan is handled by a third-party payment processor).

How we use your information

To provide and operate StatsDeck, answer your questions accurately, keep the service secure and reliable, and — only if you opt in — to send you occasional product updates. Transactional messages related to your account may be sent regardless of marketing opt-in.

Marketing email

We will only send marketing or promotional email if you explicitly opt in. You can withdraw consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in any such email.

Data sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us run StatsDeck (for example, hosting, authentication, and email delivery), and only as needed to provide the service. NFL statistics are sourced from nflverse (licensed CC-BY 4.0).

Data retention & your choices

We keep your account information while your account is active. You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
