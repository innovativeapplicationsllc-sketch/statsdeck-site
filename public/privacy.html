Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

StatsDeck ("we," "our") is an add-on (an MCP connector) for Claude that helps you analyze players, weigh matchups, and make smarter fantasy football decisions using NFL statistics and, if you connect one, your fantasy league data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, where we store it, how long we keep it, who we share it with, and how to contact us.

Signing in

You sign in through single sign-on, handled by our authentication provider, Clerk, which manages the login and holds your account email. StatsDeck uses only a pseudonymous account identifier to recognize you across sessions — it does not read, use, or store your email address.

What we collect

  • Account identifier. A pseudonymous identifier from your sign-in, used to recognize you across sessions and to associate your saved settings with you.
  • Activity timestamps. We record when you use StatsDeck, associated with your account identifier, to count active users and keep the service reliable. We do not record what you ask, which features or tools you use, or any of your conversation content.
  • League data you choose to connect. If you link a fantasy league (e.g. Sleeper), we read public league information — your roster, league scoring settings, and available players — to answer your questions, and we save this as your profile so the service remembers your league. We do not collect login passwords for these platforms.

What we don't do

  • We do not store your email address.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not post, change, or delete anything in your fantasy leagues — StatsDeck only reads.
  • We do not provide betting odds or lines, and we do not collect payment card details directly (any future paid plan would be handled by a third-party payment processor).

How we use your information

To provide and operate StatsDeck, answer your questions accurately under your league's scoring, keep the service secure and reliable, and understand how many people are actively using it. We do not use your information for advertising.

Where we store it & how long

Your saved profile — your scoring preference and any connected league's settings — is stored by our hosting provider (Railway), associated with your account identifier, and kept while you use the service. When you disconnect your league, your saved profile is deleted. Activity records are retained while the service is in use. We do not store payment details or conversation content.

Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers that help us run StatsDeck, and only as needed to operate it:

  • Clerk — handles sign-in and authentication, and holds your account email.
  • Railway — hosts the service, stores your saved profile, and keeps operational logs that include your account identifier.
  • Sleeper — when you connect a league, we read its public information; we do not send Sleeper any login or password.
  • nflverse — our source of NFL statistics (licensed CC-BY 4.0); we do not send them any user data.

We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can delete your saved profile at any time by disconnecting your league in StatsDeck. You can also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing us, and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@statsdeck.ai
