Claude is genuinely smart about football — it can reason about strategy, matchups, and trades better than most tools. But on its own, it's working from memory, without access to your league or current NFL data.

That's the gap StatsDeck fills. So Claude's reasoning runs on real, current data instead of guesses. StatsDeck also adds proprietary models and algorithms for things like projections and matchup adjustments.
