Get started

Join the private beta.

Up and running in three steps — no app to download. StatsDeck connects to Claude right from your browser, phone or desktop.

1

Add StatsDeck to Claude

New to Claude? This step gets you both at once — a Claude account and StatsDeck wired in. In Claude, open Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector, paste the StatsDeck URL, and sign in once.

https://app.statsdeck.ai/mcp

You'll sign in with Google or email — no password to create. StatsDeck only ever reads stats and league data; it never posts or changes anything in your league.
